CLEAN-UP RATES

Our clean up rates are based on cubic feet, or weight, by the ton (two thousand pounds).

We base our prices from what bigger companies charge. We know some companies like 1-800-crap, charge $920.00 to haul away 480 cubic feet, or 17.7 cubic yards of junk. That calculates to $1.91 per cubic foot, or $51.57 per cubic yard. Our prices are $1.00 per square foot, or $27.00 per cubic yard.

Hauling away something that is more heavy than bulky, we charge by the ton. Items such as scrap metal, concrete, and soil. We charge $75.00 per ton.

Disking Rates

Disking rates are calculated by miles to the job site, equipment used, and cubic yards of area to be disked.
